My New Cellphone

Jan 26th, 2011

It’s the NOKIA 1616, One of the most Fundamental phone in the recent products.

Why do I thoose this one? but not some Android phone, or Symbian phone? Serveral factors I take into account. 

The first one, which is the most fatal one, is the time I will spent on it. If I choose some Andorid phone(actually, I do have some target like samsung 5700, HTC wildfire…), I will not only take a lot of time on setuping lots of apps and refresh it to the latest OS version, but also spend timet to use these apps, then SNS, Weibo, renren…. However, if I those NOKIA 1616, there will be no such functions avalible. No SNS, no UCWeb, maybe QQ is also gone. 

According to the last term, Let me make a evaluation.Suppose I spend 15min on Sina Weibo, 15min on QQ, 15min on renren, 15min on RSS. Total time is 1 hour. Considering the preparing time for GRE, there remains 60 day left. Then there wil be 60 hours wasted on cellphone, equivallently 5 days, almost a week. This time cost is unaffordable, so I do not choose Android or Symbian 60. 

The second factor is the cost. Compared with ￥191 the price of NOKIA 1616, the cheapest Symbain60 phone is NOKIA 6120c, whose price is ￥800, nearly 4 times. Similarly, the prcies of Android phones are almost higher than ￥1000.

The third fator is power. It is said the the 1616 can be online for 6 weeks at best without charging. This sayings may be aggrandized, but for one week is a shock to me, because my service SAMSUNG i450 can will be out of power in half of a day with normal using. The power issue is the one of the deadly shortage of the Smart phone like Andorid and Symbian, which must be charge everyday.

The last reason is Updating of the Smart phone. If I spend one or two thousand yuan buying an android phone, it is definite thing that this phone is out of date. Now in the market, the newly relesed products are almost very expensive, ususly higher than ￥3000. Even though I bought the latest products with my bloodly money, newer tech is surging, higher CPU is manufacted, larger RAM is maked in half of a year. Especially in China, a unmatured smart phone envirnments, smart phone can not entirly do what thay can do. So why not save the money just wait for the real revolutionary moment, and revolutionary product. 

Some Thought About DESIGN

Dec 28th, 2010

In the lasest reading salon of Loading, Wu presented his reading experience on the book, The Non-designer’s Design Book by Robin Williams. In the public room of Institute, where we all were enthusiastic in this discussion, rather than in East Nine classroom.

In this salon, Wu introduced a lot of theories about designing, which I remember few of them except Contrast, Conhesive…

Actually I don’t like these kinds of theories, through I don’t mean I belittle the authorities of this book or these theories. I would rather believe is a kind of ARTISTIC things. All theories is just the thirsting for the form of arts, which are as meaningless as the appendix of a human body. The exact Achilles’ Ankle is the feeling occured when we saw it, which is head to discribed by common persons like us.

Nevertheless, since we design for realistic purpose, that design a produce for people to use, and we do not comperhensively provide spiritual enjoyment as well as the paintings hunged on the wall of museum or galleries.

In term of the kind of view, several principles have to be observed, before we do the artisitic part in design. With these fundamental principles, it can be used without any problem, said by my little programic selfhood.

There are principles , or concepts, or theory expressed by Robin Williams, the writer, anyway should be followed in design process:

One of the elements of  good web design   is a lack of the elements that make bad web design. If you stay away from everything listed on the page about  dorky web pages,   you’ve probably got a pretty nice web site. In addition, keep these concepts in mind:

Text
Background does not interrupt the text
Text is big enough to read, but not too big
The hierarchy of information is perfectly clear
Columns of text are narrower than in a book to make reading easier on the screen
 
Navigation
Navigation buttons and bars are easy to understand and use
Navigation is consistent throughout web site
Navigation buttons and bars provide the visitor with a clue as to where they are, what page of the site they are currently on
Frames, if used, are not obtrusive
A large site has an index or site map
 
Links
Link colors coordinate with page colors
Links are underlined so they are instantly clear to the visitor
 
Graphics
Buttons are not big and dorky
Every graphic has an alt label
Every graphic link has a matching text link
Graphics and backgrounds use browser-safe colors
Animated graphics turn off by themselves
 
General Design
Pages download quickly
First page and home page fit into 800 x 600 pixel space
All of the other pages have the immediate visual impact within 800 x 600 pixels
Good use of graphic elements (photos, subheads, pull quotes) to break up large areas of text
Every web page in the site looks like it belongs to the same site; there are repetitive elements that carry throughout the pages


These are not complicated, especially for us, who with the ambition to be an artist. Actually I think any one can be an artist, may not be accepted by the public, but by his own heart, as long as he or she designs with his or her soul.

Last night in the reading class, I read a short passage, which tells that great comics are never otherwordly and it never mystify us. They seek to accentuate the contradicitions in the social actions, not gloss over or transcend them by appeals to extrasocial symbols of divine ends, cosmic purpose, or laws of nature. 

The characters of great comics is no other than the character in which form I want to express my bloggings. Every time the signal of writing occur to me, the first thing I think is something big, holy or cosmical. But in fact I myself have master such skills or thoughts. Perhaps such stereotype forms in the 7 years’ high school writing experience. Every composition force me to write something that I never thought in my mind. So that the result of why I always hate writing worse than anything else, then gradually formed the writingphobia. 

From the first expression of New Oriental course, I found it’s like a warfare. Any minute when you are out of your mind may kick you off. Three reasons may explained.

First, the speed of the teacher is very high. Nearly our teacher speaking for the whole ninety minutes without a little rest. Also the thinking speed is especially high that I have take all my concentration on what he said. Even though, I t is still hard to entirely understand his speech. 

Second, the atmosphere is high bloody pressured. I was sitting at the last third row. I can see the whole class. And I found it is the most conscientious class I have ever take. Everyone is listening seriously to the course. This kind of atmosphere let myself go serious too.

Third, It cost my parents 1580 RMB to take this class. I must to make it worth the price, with taking my efforts.

Chaffy Dish [Huo Guo] [Fire Boiler]

Oct 4th, 2010

Chaffy Dish or Huo Guo or Fire Boiler is a famous kind of dish. On the Chaffy Dish(maybe is the accurate name), We cool raw food in the boiling soup which always been separated into spicy and not spicy in the shape of the Eight Diagrams. When the raw food has been cooled, we can put it dip in sauces which usually teel oil with mashed garlic. Then we can taste it.

Yesterday with AppleET we went to a Chaffy Dish restaurant named Chang Yi Kou Huo Guo(I found this restaurant on dianping.com which is a website for evaluating restaurants or stores. ). AppleET is a typical ChongQing girl who love the Chaffy Dish best, and she always confirm that ChongQing is the true birthland of Chaffy Dish, not SiChuan. 

It is hard to describe how the dishes tasted, but in a word, it is vary delicious. AppleET even prise that it is the best Chaffy Dish in Wuhan she has ever taken. And more seriously we two spend 171 yuan totally for this dish, which is really high cost -_-. Any way, it is really great moment in the first thirty minutes of the dish. Especially for me, it has been more than a month to taste such such amazing food. What I taken is boring breakfast and boring dinner and nothing at night. More than sixty percent I have eaten is the same, which are nothing more than Hot Dry Noodles, Fried Egg Cake, soya-bean milk in breakfast and two Liang(a unit of weight) Rice, a kind of meat dish and a vegetable dish. At night comes a fruit dish, sometimes apple, sometimes pears, sometimes mixed. It is because I am going on a diet. 

Close the end of the dish, I found I eat to much and my stomach was aching. It is such an extremely pain period that I nearly want to throw up. If I eat at that moment, any food can be the blasting fuse of the throwing up. About thirty minutes past, I just feel better. But AppleET was enjoying as happily as before and laughing at me -_-.

After the dish, we rush back to school by taxi. Then I went to a big big sleep. Even now, I can still feel the fire in my stomach. 

After all, in the third day of the long holiday, I nominally take a real relaxation for the further hard life.
